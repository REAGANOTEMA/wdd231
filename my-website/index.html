Healthy Living Hub

Plan your wellness journey with nutrition, fitness, and mental health resources.

Site Name

Healthy Living Hub

This name was chosen because the website focuses on wellness through nutrition, fitness, and mental health. It is simple, memorable, and descriptive.

Optional domain: healthylivinghub.org

Site Purpose

This website will serve as a one-stop resource for individuals seeking to improve their lifestyle. It will include recipes, beginner-friendly workouts, and mental health advice.

Scenarios

  • “What are affordable and healthy meal plans I can prepare at home?”
  • “Which beginner-friendly workouts can I do without going to the gym?”

Color Schema

#003366
#f4a261

Primary (Dark Blue): Used for headings, header, and accents.

Secondary (Warm Orange): Used for buttons, highlights, and call-to-actions.

Typography

  • Merriweather (serif): Used for headings.
  • Roboto (sans-serif): Used for body text.

Wireframe

Mobile View: Stacked layout with hero, navigation (hamburger), content, and footer.

Desktop View: Wider layout with horizontal navigation, hero text + rotating images, grid content, and footer.
